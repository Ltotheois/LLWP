Video Lessons

This is a collection of educational videos, introducing and explaining the core concepts of LLWP. They should provide a basic understanding and allow to get started. More extensive explanations can be found in the Docs (especially the shortcuts that are not explicitly given in the videos).

1. Introduction

The first video of the series gives a short introduction and presents the website, which hosts the documentation, files to download and contact data.

Show Video

2. Spectrum, Cat and Lin Files

The different file types are presented and several ways are explained, how files can be added. Additionally, project files are explained.

Show Video

3. Navigating the GUI

Ways to navigate the plots are shown together with how to alter the number of plots.

Show Video

4. Configuring the Plots

The options from the Configure Plots window are explained.

Show Video

5. Define Series

The different options to define a series are explained.

Show Video

6. Fitting and Assignment

This video explains the process of assignment with a special focus on the fitting.
